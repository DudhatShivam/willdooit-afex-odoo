Make payments to Vendors using a Foreign Currency at a Known Rate

AFEX makes the process of paying vendor bills in a foreign currency simple and efficient!

OPEN AN AFEX ACCOUNT:

Firstly you will need to set up a free, no obligation account with AFEX. - click here to register. We'll contact you to complete your account set up and answer any questions you may have about managing your global payments through AFEX. Once your account is activated, you are ready to set up your vendor's bank details and start booking foreign currency payments!

SET UP VENDORS AS BENEFICIARIES PAID BY AFEX:

  • Mark an overseas supplier as a beneficiary to be paid by AFEX
  • Enter the supplier's currency and bank account details
  • Sychronise the beneficiary details to AFEX
  • After approval by AFEX, you can book vendor bill payments to the supplier.

PAY A VENDOR BILL:

  • Register an overseas vendor bill
  • Select AFEX Payments as the payment method and obtain a quote for the foreign currency amount
  • Validate the quote to book the foreigin currency payment
  • Transfer the local currency settlement amount to AFEX to fund the payment
  • AFEX will send the payment to the vendor via SWIFT on the second business day from the date the payment is booked.
